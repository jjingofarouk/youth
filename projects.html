Our Projects

Making Impact Through Action

Sustainable Medical Waste Management

Implementing eco-friendly medical waste disposal systems in rural healthcare facilities across Uganda. This project has reduced harmful emissions while improving healthcare safety standards.

  • 15 facilities implemented
  • 50% reduction in harmful disposal methods
  • 1000+ healthcare workers trained

Climate-Health Education Program

Training young medical professionals to understand and communicate the health impacts of climate change. Our program creates a network of informed healthcare advocates.

  • 2000+ students reached
  • 25 medical schools engaged
  • 100+ workshops conducted

Green Healthcare Facilities

Converting healthcare facilities to renewable energy sources and implementing energy-efficient practices. This project reduces operational costs while minimizing environmental impact.

  • 10 solar-powered clinics
  • 30% average energy cost reduction
  • 500 tons CO2 emissions prevented
