About Youth4GreenUganda

Empowering Young Medical Professionals for Climate Action

Our Story

Youth4GreenUganda emerged from the frontlines of healthcare in Uganda, where young medical professionals witnessed the devastating health impacts of climate change. Founded by healthcare practitioners, our initiative bridges the gap between medical expertise and environmental action.

Mission

To mobilize Uganda's youth and medical community in creating sustainable healthcare practices while combating climate change through education, research, and direct action.

Vision

A Uganda where healthcare and environmental sustainability work hand in hand, led by an empowered youth movement.
